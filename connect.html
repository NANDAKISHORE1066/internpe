Connect 4 Game

Game Rules

  • The game is played on a grid that's 7 columns by 6 rows.
  • Players take turns dropping one of their discs from the top into any of the seven columns.
  • The piece falls straight down, occupying the lowest available space within the column.
  • The objective is to be the first to form a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal line of four of one's own discs.
  • The game ends when there is a 4-in-a-row or a stalemate if the board is full and no player has a 4-in-a-row.
